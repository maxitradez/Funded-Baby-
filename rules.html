Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I hit the max drawdown? ▼
Your account is automatically closed and the challenge ends. You will need to purchase a new challenge to try again. The original challenge fee is not refunded if you breach a risk rule — only upon successful funding.
How is the daily drawdown calculated? ▼
Daily drawdown is based on the balance at the start of each trading day (midnight server time, EAT+0). If your equity drops more than the daily limit below that starting balance at any point during the day — including on open floating positions — the limit is breached.
When does the clock start on minimum trading days? ▼
A "trading day" is any day where you have at least one fully closed trade. Days where you only open positions (no closes) do not count. The counter starts from your first closed trade after account activation.
Can I use an EA (automated trading bot)? ▼
Yes — EAs are allowed as long as they don't use martingale, grid doubling, or latency arbitrage. All trades must reflect genuine market analysis. We reserve the right to review any EA strategy if it raises compliance flags.
What happens after I pass the challenge? ▼
Our team reviews your account within 24–48 hours. If all rules are met and trading is consistent, you receive your funded live account login credentials via email. You can then start trading real capital and request payouts.
Is the challenge fee refunded? ▼
Yes — your challenge fee is fully refunded when you receive your first payout from your funded account. It is not refunded if you fail the challenge by breaching a risk rule.
What payment methods are accepted for payouts? ▼
We pay out via M-Pesa (Kenya & East Africa), bank transfer, and USDT crypto (TRC-20). All payouts are processed within 48 hours of a valid request. You can choose your preferred method in your profile settings.
