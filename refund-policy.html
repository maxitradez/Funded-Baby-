Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Challenge fees are generally non-refundable once a challenge has commenced. Please review this policy fully before purchasing.

Challenge Fee Refunds

Upon successfully completing an evaluation and being approved for a funded account, your one-time challenge fee is refunded in full as part of your first payout. This applies to all programs: Accelerator, Endurance, and Elite Path.

Pre-Commencement Cancellations

If you have purchased a challenge but have not yet placed a trade, you may request a full refund within 72 hours of purchase. After this window, or after the first trade is placed, fees become non-refundable.

Non-Refundable Circumstances

  • Challenge fee after the first trade has been placed
  • Failed challenges (missed profit target or breached drawdown)
  • Account disqualification due to rule violations
  • Fees older than 72 hours where no trade has been placed

Technical Issues

If a technical failure on our platform materially affects your ability to trade, please contact support immediately. We will investigate and, where appropriate, reset the affected challenge period or issue a credit.

How to Request a Refund

Contact us within the applicable window via:

  • WhatsApp: +254 718 828 633
  • Instagram: @maxi.trades

Include your account email, challenge type, and transaction reference. Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
