🔒 Our Commitment

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains exactly what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights over it. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

01 Introduction

Funded Baby ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our website, platform, and services.

This policy applies to all users of our platform, including visitors, registered traders, challenge participants, and funded account holders. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

03 How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery — Processing challenge purchases, managing accounts, calculating performance, processing payouts
  • Communication — Sending account updates, payout confirmations, support responses, and important notices
  • Compliance Monitoring — Reviewing trading activity for rule violations and prohibited conduct
  • Identity Verification — Confirming eligibility and preventing fraud
  • Platform Improvement — Analysing usage to improve our features and services
  • Legal Obligations — Meeting applicable legal and regulatory requirements
  • Security — Detecting and preventing unauthorized access or fraudulent activity

04 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Payment Processors — M-Pesa (Safaricom), card payment providers, and crypto networks to process transactions
  • Trading Platform Providers — MetaTrader and similar platforms to provide challenge accounts
  • Legal Authorities — When required by law, court order, or regulatory authority
  • Business Successors — In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of the business

All third parties are required to handle your data securely and only for specified purposes.

05 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Password hashing and salting for stored credentials
  • Access controls limiting who can view personal data
  • Regular security reviews and updates

While we take all reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential.

06 Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our platform. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies — Required for the platform to function (login sessions, security tokens)
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand how users interact with the platform (anonymous data only)
  • Preference Cookies — Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may impact platform functionality.

07 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restriction — Request we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp (+254 718 828 633) or our contact form. We will respond within 30 days.

08 Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and meet legal obligations. Specifically:

  • Active account holders — For the duration of your account plus 3 years
  • Closed accounts — Up to 5 years for financial/legal compliance
  • Trading records — Minimum 5 years for audit purposes
  • Payment records — As required by applicable financial regulations

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

09 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor has registered, we will immediately delete their data and account.

10 Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify registered users via email and post a notice on the platform. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
